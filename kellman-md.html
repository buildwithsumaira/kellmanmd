    Everything you need to know before your first visit with Dr. Kellman.

    What makes Dr. Kellman different from other functional medicine doctors? +
    Dr. Kellman pioneered the field of Microbiome Medicine — he coined the term and developed its clinical application before most physicians had heard of it. His combination of deep diagnostic expertise, exclusive therapeutic technologies (NovoTHOR, PEMF, Magnesphere, IV Ozone), and the ability to connect complex, seemingly unrelated symptoms makes him uniquely positioned to help patients others cannot.
    I've been to many doctors and no one has found the cause. Can Dr. Kellman help? +
    This is exactly who Dr. Kellman specializes in. The majority of his patients come to him after years of suffering, multiple misdiagnoses, or being told nothing is wrong. Using advanced testing including microbiome analysis, TRH thyroid stimulation, genetics, and inflammatory markers — he consistently finds what standard medicine misses.
    What is Microbiome Medicine? +
    Microbiome Medicine is a field Dr. Kellman pioneered, recognizing the gut's ecosystem of bacteria as a central orchestrator of virtually all the body's systems. Almost every chronic disease — from autoimmune conditions to cognitive decline to thyroid disease — has a microbiome component. By healing the microbiome first, the body begins to heal itself naturally and foundationally.

    Do you treat patients from outside New York? +
    Yes. Dr. Kellman has patients from across the United States and internationally. Many patients travel to see him specifically because his level of expertise and diagnostic capability is unavailable in their home regions. We can discuss remote consultation options where clinically appropriate.
    What is the TRH Stimulation Test? +
    The TRH Stimulation Test is one of the most important diagnostic tools for thyroid disease — and one that most doctors, even in functional medicine, do not offer. Standard thyroid blood panels frequently miss low thyroid function. Dr. Kellman's precise use of the TRH test has transformed the lives of countless patients who were told their thyroid was "normal" but continued to suffer.
